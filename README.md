# Hola 👋

I'm an engineering manager and senior software engineer with 15+ years building and leading the systems that keep fintech and insurtech companies safe, solvent, and shipping. My specialty is the unglamorous-but-mission-critical core: real-time fraud detection, credit risk rule engines, PLD/FT and KYC/AML compliance pipelines, and the async infrastructure that has to stay up under real production load.

  Most recently at Baubap — Mexico's largest regulated mobile microlender (SOFOM ENR) — I built and owned the real-time fraud monitoring platform end-to-end: signal ingestion, rule evaluation, DataDog-instrumented dashboards, sub-minute detection latency, and a steady drop in false positives. I designed the sistema automatizado PLD that handles CNBV-mandated monitoreo transaccional, ROR/ROI/ROIP generation, fraccionamiento detection, and SITI PLD reporting — the technical backbone the Oficial de Cumplimiento and the Dictamen Técnico PLD audit rely on. I also built the KYC/AML stack: listas restrictivas screening (UIF, OFAC, ONU), PEP and Enhanced KYC workflows, and the matriz de riesgo tooling that classifies clients under the Enfoque Basado en Riesgos framework.

  Before that, I led a team of five engineers at Koffie Financial (commercial trucking insurtech), where I designed a DAG-based rating engine with a custom actuarial DSL, integrated Samsara telematics into underwriting, and launched the first credit card purpose-built for US trucking fleets. Earlier I founded and scaled a 25-person cross-border engineering office from a 2-person pilot for Eastridge Workforce Solutions.

  The thread across all of it: I'm a hands-on manager. I code, I architect, I run the sprint, I do the 1:1s, and I take extreme ownership of whether the business outcome actually moved. I've grown engineers from mid to senior, run distributed teams across time zones, and shipped in high-stakes regulated environments where a bad release costs real money — or real regulatory exposure.

  Native Spanish, C2 English. Based in Tijuana, comfortable fully remote or hybrid. Always interested in conversations with teams building consequential financial infrastructure — especially anything serving Latin America or the Latin diaspora, where I think there's still a generation of product yet to be built.
